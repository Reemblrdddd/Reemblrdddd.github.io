Pancakes

Pancakes are one of the greatest things to ever touch earth . It hold great history and taste.It wasnt sweet like the pancakes today but just a mixture of smashed nuts mixed with water to make a batter. This was then put on a hot rock to bake it. Pancakes have been popular due to how easy they are to make. Simply make a flour-liquid mixture and pour onto a hot surface. Each country being able to make the pancake original to itsself.

Source:Smithsonian magazine

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Baking powder

Directions

  1. Combine dry Ingredients
  2. add wet ingredients in the middle
  3. Mix
  4. Pour batter onto pan
  5. Flip when bubbles form
  6. Take out after a similar amount of time has passed from when you flipped the pancake.
  7. Consume
